## Survey Purpose
Validate the "Maria Rodriguez" tenant persona and understand tenant experiences, pain points, and preferences for maintenance reporting.

## Target Participants
- 5-10 tenants from residential buildings
- Mix of ages, tech comfort levels, building types
- Recent experience with maintenance requests (within last 6 months)

## Survey Distribution Methods
1. **Email survey** to building mailing lists
2. **Printed survey** in building common areas
3. **In-person interviews** (15-20 minutes)
4. **Online form** (Google Forms, Typeform, etc.)

## Survey Questions (15-20 minutes total)

### Section 1: Demographics & Building Information

1. **What type of building do you live in?**
   - [ ] Apartment complex (200+ units)
   - [ ] Small apartment building (< 50 units)
   - [ ] Condominium
   - [ ] Townhouse
   - [ ] Single-family home (rental)
   - [ ] Other: _________

2. **How long have you lived in your current residence?**
   - [ ] Less than 6 months
   - [ ] 6 months - 1 year
   - [ ] 1-3 years
   - [ ] 3-5 years
   - [ ] More than 5 years

3. **What's your age range?**
   - [ ] 18-24
   - [ ] 25-34
   - [ ] 35-44

### Section 2: Current Maintenance Experience

## Survey Distribution Plan

### Online Version (Google Forms/Typeform)
- Create digital form with above questions
- Share via:
  - Building newsletter/email list
  - Social media building groups
  - QR codes in common areas
- Offer incentive: $5 coffee gift card for completion

### Printed Version
- Print double-sided with large font
- Place in:
  - Mailroom
  - Laundry room
  - Elevators
  - Community bulletin board
- Include drop box for completed surveys
- Offer incentive: Entry into $50 gift card drawing

### In-Person Interviews
- Schedule 15-minute sessions
- Conduct in building common areas
- Use tablet to record responses
- Offer immediate incentive ($5 coffee card)

## Analysis Plan

### Quantitative Analysis:
1. **Response rates** by distribution method
2. **Demographic breakdown** of respondents
3. **Frequency analysis** of multiple-choice responses
4. **Cross-tabulation** (e.g., age vs. tech comfort vs. preferences)

### Qualitative Analysis:
1. **Thematic analysis** of open-ended responses
2. **Pain point categorization**
3. **Feature request prioritization**
4. **Persona validation** against "Maria Rodriguez"

### Success Metrics:
- **Goal:** 5-10 completed surveys
- **Minimum:** 3 surveys with complete data
- **Quality:** At least 50% provide open-ended responses
